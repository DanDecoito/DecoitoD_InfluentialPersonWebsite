Influence

I've always loved to read and spent countless hours pouring over books searching for new thoughts and ideas. Out of everything that I have ever read the writings that had the greatest effect on shaping my perspective of the world was the words of Philip K Dick. In his novel Valis, Dick puts forth the notion that most people accept reality without forumlating any thoughts themselves. Instead we accept the narative of exsitance provided to us by our instutions, whether religous or academic. Instead he proposes that we try to build an understand of reality bassed of our own experiences. In this endevaour we are to probe deep into our misunderstanding and develop our own thoughts. This isnt meant to discredit the work of everyone before us. On the contrary, he encourages us to examine the work more deeply and to understand the essnece of the argument presented. Then with carful scrutiny disect and understand the work and see how it applies to the reality that you've experienced. When completeld we will start to build a framework of understanding within our subjective reality which becomes our belief.
